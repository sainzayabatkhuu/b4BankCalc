These calculators, or the results generated, do not constitute any agreement by NAB to provide credit.

This calculator is designed to show you what size loan your rental payments would service, at the interest rate that you have selected in the tool. It does not take into account other factors such as your income, other debt repayments, general living expenses and other credit assessment criteria (including serviceability assessment). It is not designed to provide an indication of borrowing power or loan approval. Lending by NAB is subject to NAB\'s normal lending criteria.

The default monthly rent amount of $1,235 has been used as it represents the median monthly rent in Australia from the 2011 census. Interest rate quoted is NAB’s standard variable rate as at May 26, 2015. The default loan term of 30 years represents the longest loan term NAB may offer as part of its home loan products.

Terms, conditions, fees, charges and eligibility criteria apply. Comparison rates are based on a loan of $150,000 over a term of 25 years WARNING: This comparison rate applies only to the example or examples given. Different amounts and terms will result in different comparison rates. Costs such as redraw fees or early repayment fees, and cost savings such as fee waivers, are not included in the comparison rate but may influence the cost of the loan.
